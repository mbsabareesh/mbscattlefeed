The economic boom in our country has brought about a silent revolution in the form of increased per capita nutrition consumption among the Indian population. A significant contributor to this upward trend in nutrition is the availability of animal protein and dairy products. As a result, there is a growing public demand for high-quality meat, eggs, and milk, presenting a significant challenge to meet this demand. The responsibility of providing quality, wholesome food lies heavily on the shoulders of our dairy and poultry farmers.

The success of these farmers in meeting the nation's nutritional needs is largely dependent on the quality of feed provided to their animals. This is where MBS Cattle Feed plays a pivotal role. Renowned for its superior quality and balanced nutrition, MBS Cattle Feed ensures that livestock receive the essential nutrients they need to thrive. By supporting the health and productivity of dairy and poultry animals, MBS Cattle Feed is helping farmers across the country meet the rising demand for high-quality animal products, thereby contributing to the overall nutritional upliftment of the population.

At MBS Cattle Feed, we are committed to providing high-quality feed solutions tailored to meet the nutritional needs of your livestock. With our extensive range of products, we cater to various segments, including milch cows, buffaloes, calves, sheep, goats, broilers, layers, nattukozhi (country chickens), and quails.

As part of our commitment to innovation and leadership in the Indian Animal Feed Industry, we have recently diversified our offerings. We are now stepping into the Agri-input business and commodity trading, which will enable us to deliver a broader spectrum of services to our customers. Our goal is to continue leading the industry by offering comprehensive solutions that meet the evolving needs of farmers across India.

In Tamil Nadu, MBS Cattle Feed has taken a significant step by entering the "Broiler Integration" segment. This move allows us to optimize opportunities within the poultry industry and reinforces our mission to emerge as a leader in the market.
