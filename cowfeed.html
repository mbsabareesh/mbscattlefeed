Dairy cows require a meticulously balanced diet to ensure optimal milk production, maintain body health, and support overall well-being. The quality of cattle feed plays a pivotal role in achieving these goals, and it must be provided at optimal levels to match the production demands of the animals.

The quantity and quality of milk produced by dairy cows are heavily influenced by their diet and feeding practices. The feed provided should be carefully adjusted based on the cow's milk yield, weight, environmental conditions, and activity levels.

In recent times, many dairy farmers have shifted towards using compound cattle feed alongside dry fodder and green grass as a staple part of their dairy nutrition regimen. This approach not only enhances productivity but also helps in optimizing milk production costs and increasing overall profitability.

MBS Cattle Feed offers a range of balanced feed formulations tailored to meet the needs of dairy cows, whether they are medium or high milk yielders, dry, or working animals. By incorporating MBS Cattle Feed into your dairy operation, you can ensure that your cows receive the nourishment they need for peak performance, leading to better milk production and improved financial outcomes.

MBS CATTEL FEED Special Pellet is designed for animals yielding upto 10 litres of milk in a day. Available in 20Kg, 50Kg and 70Kg PP bags.
