Sheep and goats are not just valuable assets for farmers; they are a low-input, moderate-income source that can deliver even greater returns with the right care. Though they may seem easy to rear, these animals can yield significantly better results when managed scientifically. A crucial aspect of this scientific approach is proper nutrition.

MBS Cattle Feed is specially formulated to meet the nutritional needs of sheep and goats, ensuring they receive the right balance of vitamins, minerals, and energy. By incorporating MBS Cattle Feed into your feeding regimen, you’ll be supporting the overall health and productivity of your livestock, leading to higher yields and increased income.

MBS CATTEL FEED sheep & goat feed is recommended from 200 gms to 250 gms per day in general depending on the size and body weight of the animal along with sufficient green and dry fodders. The feed is available in pellet form in 20Kg and 50Kg PP bags.
