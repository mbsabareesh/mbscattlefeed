Poultry farming in India has seen a remarkable transformation, evolving from small-scale backyard operations with fewer than a hundred birds to a highly organized, techno-commercial industry housing thousands or even lakhs of birds over the last three decades. Today, India proudly ranks as the world's third-largest egg producer and the fourth-largest in chicken meat production. This success is primarily driven by the growth of large-scale commercial poultry operations.

To support and enhance the productivity of these traditional backyard farms, MBS Cattle Company has introduced Hen Feed. This specialized feed is formulated to meet the nutritional needs of desi birds like Nattukozhi, ensuring they remain healthy, robust, and capable of producing high-quality meat and eggs. Heen Feed combines the best of traditional feeding practices with modern nutritional science, offering a balanced diet that supports the growth and well-being of these birds.

By choosing Hen Feed, farmers can ensure their Nattukozhi chickens receive the essential nutrients they need, preserving the rich heritage of backyard poultry farming while contributing to the overall poultry industry in India. MBS Cattle Company remains committed to supporting farmers of all scales, from large commercial operations to small backyard farms, with quality feed solutions that drive success.

From day 1 to the age of marketing, that is by 16 weeks, the Nattukozhi will consume around 4 to 5Kgs of feed and attains a body weight of 1.3 to 2.5Kgs.
