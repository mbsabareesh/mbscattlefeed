Japanese quails are robust and cost-effective birds to raise, primarily valued for their eggs and meat. At MBS Company, we recognize the unique demands of quail farming and are dedicated to providing specialized solutions for quail nutrition.

To maximize the benefits of quail farming, it's crucial to offer a balanced feed ration, as feed accounts for 60-70% of the total farm investment. Balanced nutrition is essential for keeping quails in optimal health, supporting their growth, and ensuring high egg production.

With MBS Quail Feed, you can rest assured that your quail will receive the highest quality nutrition, leading to better health, increased egg production, and faster growth. Trust MBS to support your quail farming success with feeds designed for optimal results.
